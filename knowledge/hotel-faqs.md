# Hotel Knowledge Base — Oyugis Le Grand Hotel

## Check-in and Check-out Times
Check-in is from 2:00 PM. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in may be available subject to room availability — please ask at reception. Late check-out until 2:00 PM can be arranged for a small fee.

## Room Types and Rates
We offer Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms, Junior Suites, and the Presidential Suite. Rates vary by season and room type. Please contact the front desk or visit our website for current pricing. All rates include complimentary breakfast for two guests.

## Breakfast and Restaurant
Breakfast is served from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM in the main restaurant. Our restaurant is open for lunch from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM and dinner from 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM. We serve a mix of Kenyan and international cuisine.

## Amenities
Our hotel features a swimming pool (open 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM), a fitness center (open 24 hours), free high-speed WiFi throughout the property, air conditioning in all rooms, flat-screen TVs, room service from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, laundry and dry cleaning services, airport transfers (advance booking required), and a conference room for up to 50 guests.

## Parking
Complimentary secure parking is available for all hotel guests. Our parking area is monitored 24/7. Please let reception know if you require parking for an oversized vehicle.

## Booking and Reservations
Bookings can be made by phone, email, or through our website. We require a valid credit card or M-Pesa payment to secure a reservation. Cancellations made more than 48 hours before arrival are fully refunded. Same-day cancellations are charged one night's stay.

## Cancellation Policy
Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before your scheduled arrival to receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged the full reservation amount.

## Location and Directions
We are located in Oyugis town, Homabay County, Kenya. From Kisumu, take the Kisii Road south for approximately 70 km. From Nairobi, take the Nairobi-Kisumu Highway and head towards Oyugis. GPS coordinates are available on our website. We are happy to arrange airport transfers from Kisumu Airport.

## Conference and Events
Our conference room accommodates up to 50 guests with full audio-visual equipment, projector, and whiteboard. We offer day packages and full-day packages including meals. Please contact us in advance to check availability and receive a quote for corporate events, weddings, or private functions.

## WiFi Access
Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel. The network name and password are provided at check-in. If you experience any connectivity issues, please contact the front desk.

## Room Service
Room service is available from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. A full menu is available in your room. Please allow 30–45 minutes for delivery during peak hours.

## Payments Accepted
We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and cash in Kenyan Shillings. For large payments, we recommend electronic transfer. All prices are listed in Kenyan Shillings (KES).

## Pets Policy
We regret that pets are not permitted on the property, with the exception of certified guide dogs or service animals. Please notify us in advance if you will be accompanied by a service animal.

## Accessibility
Our ground floor rooms are accessible for guests with mobility requirements. Please inform us at the time of booking if you require an accessible room or any special assistance.
